RaysChatBot Commands

Type these in chat. Everything comes straight from the live shiny-hunt.

[switch] = optional, a number 1–3. Leave it off to see all switches at once. <arg> = required. Viewer commands have a 10s cooldown. Mods bypass it. 🎮 New: you can play the game from chat! How Chat Plays works →

Live stats

Ask the bot anything about the current hunt.

!count [switch]

Current encounter counts. Omit the switch for a one-line total of all three; add one for the per-Pokémon breakdown.

e.g. !count · !count 2

!odds [switch]

The current shiny odds, including the hunt method and whether the Shiny Charm is active.

e.g. !odds · !odds 1

!target [switch]

Which Pokémon is currently being hunted on each switch.

e.g. !target · !target 3

!luck [switch]

How lucky (or cursed) the current hunt is running compared to the expected odds.

e.g. !luck · !luck 2

!eta [switch]

Estimated time until a shiny at the current encounter rate, plus the encounters-per-hour pace.

e.g. !eta · !eta 1

!uptime [switch]

How long the current hunt has been running and how many encounters it has gained this session.

e.g. !uptime · !uptime 3

History & records

!lastshiny

The most recent shiny caught — name, date, the encounter count it took, and the method.

e.g. !lastshiny

!record

Lifetime records: total shinies, lifetime encounters, the longest hunt, and the fastest shiny.

e.g. !record

!catchinfo <pokemon>

When a specific Pokémon was caught and how many encounters it took.

e.g. !catchinfo ralts

Play along

Get in on the hunt yourself.

!p <buttons>

Chat Plays! Press buttons in Pokémon LeafGreen right from chat — add a number to hold a button for that many seconds, and chain up to 5. Full how-to, button list & rules →

e.g. !p a · !p up 2 · !p up 2 a b

!points

Your stream points balance. You earn points just by hanging out in chat — more ways to use them coming soon.

e.g. !points

!guess <pokemon> <encounters>

Predict how many encounters an active hunt will take. The closest guess is crowned when the shiny is found. One guess per hunt — it locks in, so choose carefully.

e.g. !guess rayquaza 1000

!claim

Were you here when a shiny hit? Claim it within 10 minutes to get credited on the leaderboard.

e.g. !claim

!claims

The shiny-claim leaderboard — who has been present for the most shinies.

e.g. !claims
